About MPI

The only one of its kind in Canada, the Media & the Public Interest (MPI) program connects issues of communication and media to social justice issues such as democracy, equity, class, race, and gender. MPI students think critically about how media and technology has and can be used to communicate, empower, or hinder efforts to achieve equality and social justice.

Through a combination of coursework and experiential learning opportunities, students will meaningfully engage with the issues affecting their local and global communities.

Some of the ideas, topics, and issues that MPI explores include

  • Media coverage of social movements
  • Activism and independent media
  • Documentary media and issues of representation
    •
  • The communication of human rights
  • Social media and democracy
  • Censorship and free expression
