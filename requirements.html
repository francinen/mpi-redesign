Admissions & Degree Requirements

Enrolment in all modules in Media and the Public Interest is limited to a maximum of 20 students per year. Meeting the minimum requirements does not guarantee that students wishing to enter any module in Media and the Public Interest will be offered enrolment.

Scroll down to view the requirements for the Honors Specialization in MPI and the MPI Major, then visit the registration page to find out how to apply.

  • Honors Specialization in MPI

    Completion of all first-year requirements with no failures. Students must have an average of at least 72% in 4.0 principal courses, with no mark in any principal course below 60%, and including a minimum grade of 70% in each of MIT 1200F/G, 1500A/B and 1700F/G.

    • Second year: MIT 2000F/G, 2100F/G, 2200F/G, 2500A/B, 2152F/G, Writing 2121F/G
    • Third year: MIT 3000A/B, 3100F/G, MIT 3901F/G, 3902F/G
    • Fourth year: MIT 4999F/G and 0.5 course from MIT courses numbered 4030F/G-4039F/G
    • 1.0 additional course in MIT at the 3000 level or above
    • 1.5 courses chosen from the approved list of MPI elective courses at the 2000 level or above
    • 0.5 course in MIT at the 2000 level or above

  • MPI Major

    Completion of all first-year requirements with no failures. Students must have an average of at least 68% in 4.0 principal courses, including a minimum grade of 65% in each of MIT 1200F/G, 1500F/G and 1700F/G.

    • Second year: MIT 2000F/G, 2100F/G, 2200F/G, 2500A/B, 2152F/G, Writing 2121F/G
    • Third year: MIT 3000A/B, 3100F/G, MIT 3901F/G, 3902F/G
    • Fourth year: MIT 4999F/G
    • 2.0 courses chosen from the approved list of MPI elective courses at the 2000 level or above
    • 0.5 additional MIT course at the 3000 level
