How to Apply

  1. Make sure you meet all admissions requirements

  2. Complete the registration form below to receive details about upcoming information sessions

  3. Attend an information session to obtain an application form

  4. Submit your application form before the Intent to Register deadline

Please note that a maximum of 20 students are admitted into the program each year.

Meeting the minimum requirements does not guarantee that students wishing to enter any module will be offered enrolment.
